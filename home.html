About us


Dr.Shaina passed away at a very young age.She had great concern for the Physically Differently Abled and Mentally Handicapped Persons.Dr.Shaina Memorial Trust has been founded in with the objective of serving the Differently Abled.To realize this objective "Karunya Special School" has been started in 2017 at Ambalpady,Udupi in a rented house.We have a strong conviction to realize and Materialize the noble thought of Late.Dr.Shaina .



Objective

The ultimate objectve of Dr.Shaina Trust is to bring the Mentally Retarded,Cerebral Palsy,Multiple Disabled,Autism and learning disabled person(children) into the main stream of the society.



Mission

Facilitate the Differently Abled Persons who are deprived of opportunities of regular schooling and provide them every opportunities acquire Special Education in accordance with their requirements.



Vission

Provide necessary life Skill Education to the Special children and train them to acquire such life skills to handle themselves independently and enable them to get in to the main stream of the society.
